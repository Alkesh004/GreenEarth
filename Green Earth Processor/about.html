What We Do

Green Earth Processors Products holds large farmlands and grow and process sugarcane, paddy and cotton. It is the member of sugarcane growers cooperative society. Along with this, it also has animal husbandry business and produce fruits, vegetables and flowers in Green House Plant with 126 hectares of land.

It has 22 full time employees taking care of agriculture and green house products at their farm. It owns majority of the agriculture machinery like Tractor, Harvester, Bailer, Thrasher Computerized plough, Water pumping machines etc.
